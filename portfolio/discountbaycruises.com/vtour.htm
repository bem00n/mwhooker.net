Scenic San Francisco:
Your voyage will introduce you to the most spectacular and breathtaking sights of San Francisco - the majestic Golden Gate Bridge, mystery-shrouded Alcatraz Island, and many views of the beautiful San Francisco Cityscape. Click on the pictures for a larger view.		 Here you can see the famous Coit Tower. The tower was built in 1933 with funding from philanthropist Lillie Hitchcock Coit. It was designed to resemble a fire hose in recognition of the valor of the firefighters during the 1906 earthquake.
The tour offers an excellent view of Alcatraz Island from all angles. See the ominous guard tower and the prison up close. See the red underbelly of the famous Golden Gate Bridge where a safety net was strung during construction. The net saved the lives of 19 men who later became known as the "Half-Way-to-Hell club".
Your first stop on the way to your tour, the ticket office. Located at the end of Taylor Street on pier 43 1/2, you can redeem your voucher here. The Marina district as well as the Transamerica Pyramid, Bank of America building, and a better part of the Financial district.
