some helpful sites
     Below are a list of sites that we thought might be useful. This list is
     always growing so keep an eye out for updates.		    
     
San Francisco Convention & Visitors Bureau - General San Francisco travel  information. Provides info on using MUNI (public transportation); and how to travel around the city, including Alcatraz and Fisherman's Warf.

Pier 45 — Fisherman's Wharf San Francisco - For the full San Francisco experience, visit this site. They offer historic tours of Fisherman's Warf, including the World War II era Liberty Ship, "Jeremiah O'Brien"; and the USS Pampanito submarine. Weather with a group or alone you'll enjoy a fun and educational partially guided tour.
